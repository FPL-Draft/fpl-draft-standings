Scoring Rules

The point of the scoring system is to also keep account of the total points to remove some randomness and luck and introduce more long-term strategic thinking. Scoring is calculated by following the formulas* below:

H2H Score: 8 - H2H Rank

TP Score: (8 - TP Rank) + (9 - TP Rank)/10
